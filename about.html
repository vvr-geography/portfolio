Hello everyone!

My name is Vignesh Ramachandran. I'm a graduate student and human geographer at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My research interests include social theory, asian-american studies, the south asian diaspora, and social movements. My current work relates to post-9/11 political organizing in South Asian communities in New York City and its relation to contemporary working class social movements in the city. To follow along with my work, please follow me on Twitter: @_viggy !
