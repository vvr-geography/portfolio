A Carnivalesque View of the World

I wanted to make a map that embraced the tradition of Trinidadian carnival and Caribbean sunshine. Drawing inspiration from vintage tourism posters for carnival in Trinidad and Tobago, as well as photos of extravagant carnival mas, I settled on a color palette with bright yellows and oranges, accented with magenta and turquoise to evoke the convivial and jovial spirit of carnival. Finally, I wanted to connect the map to the carnivalesque spirit of Trindidadian playwright Mustapha Matura's drama Playboy of the West Indies, the story of a failed Oedipal murder and one where all social hierarchy is overturned.
